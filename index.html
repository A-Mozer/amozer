Expertise

My main interest is to use DNA to fight illegal wildlife trade,
but wildlife forensics is a broad field with many applications.

  • Ivory identification

    Morphological identification of ivory and ivory substitutes.

  • Species identification

    Molecular identification of species using mitochondrial barcoding.

  • Individual identification

    Genetic fingerprint to prove poaching and connect crime scences.

  • Relationship testing

    Parentgage and offspring to detect laundering of wild animals.

  • Geographic assignment

    Determine the country of origin of a seizure sample based on a DNA profile database.

  • SNPSTR Marker

    Designing and validating SNPSTR marker based on NGS technology.
